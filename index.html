Exotic dance moves

Yes, you are right! As part of my post-doctorate work, I did study exotic tribal dance. To answer your question, my favorite dance are as follows, with definitions:

Polymesian chicken dance

A little known but very influential dance dating back as far as 300BC, a whole village would dance around in a circle like chickens, to encourage their livestock to be "fruitful"

Icelandic brownian shuffle

Before the icelanders developed fire as a means of getting warm, they used to practice this dance, which involved huddling close togeter on the floor, and shuffling their bodie around in imperceptibly tiny, very rapid movement. One of my fellow student used to say he tought this dance inspired mordern styles such as Twerking.

Artic robot dance

An interesting example of historic misinformation, English explores in the 1960 belived to have discovered a new dance style charaterised by "robotic",stilted their bodies practiced by inhabitants of Northern Alaska and Canada. Later it was discovered that they were just moving like this because they were cold.
